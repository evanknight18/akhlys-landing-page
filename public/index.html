About Us

Akhlys is a black metal band known for their atmospheric and dark musical style. The band was formed by Naas Alcameth, who is also associated with other projects like Nightbringer and Bestia Arcana. Akhlys' music often delves into themes of dreams, nightmares, and the occult, creating a haunting and immersive listening experience. Their discography includes albums such as "The Dreaming I" and "Melinoë," which have been well-received in the black metal community for their complex compositions and evocative atmospheres.

Discography

  • Supplication (2009)
  • Dreaming I (2015)
  • Melinoë (2020)
  • House of the Black Geminus (2024)
